Our Process

Our tailored venture coaching program starts with a situational understanding, then continues to a divergent-incubate-convergent planning process.

1. Situational Assessment (2h)

All meaningful progress start with situational awareness. Francis works with you to understand the current stage of the company, market segment, competitive landscape, and obstacles. Then we collaboratively identify the current business playbook and its associated set of pain points.
  • Disciplined Entrepreneurship framework by Martin Trust Center, MIT
  • Playbook analysis using the Ten Types framework by Deloitte.

2. Brand Clarity and Focus Workshop (4h)

Francis conducts a rigorous, purpose-centric workshop in which we (re)define your company, culture, and story to bring clarity to your brand. Existing pain points are reassessed in a renewed frame of reference. We hold an expansive mindset, and continue to explore possibilities at this stage.
  • Market positioning revisited with "why".
  • Build detailed customer personas of all relevant segments.
  • Revisit competitve landscape, including indirect competition.
  • Establish mission statement and unique value proposition.
  • Generate playbooks and growth strategies to test.

3. Productivty and Knowledge Management Workshop (4h)

Francis introduces tools to help manage the influx of new initiatives with focus. Here we tweak the detailed implementation of these workflows to suit your situation. You may already have some exposure to some of these systems.
  • Getting Things Done (GTD), pioneered by David Allen.
  • Build a Second Brain (BASB), an extension of Zettlekasten by Tiago Forte.
  • Custom journal templates and checklists to reinforce habits.
  • Create support systems of accountability.

4. Plan and Commit to Next Steps (2h)

Convergent session to apply the updated company missions and promising playbooks to generate action items for the next 90-days. Next actions are committed with your accountability support system. We wrap the program with exploration of value-add services that may fit your situation, such as:
  • Investor introductions and pitch coaching.
  • Public-private fund opportunities and grant writing guidance.
  • Transformation case study and content opportunities.
